Common Questions

Answers to your most asked questions

Do I need a referral to register at Magnificent God Maternity Center?
+

No referral is needed. You can walk in or book an appointment directly via our website, phone, or WhatsApp. We welcome all patients, whether referred or self-registering.

When should I register for antenatal care?
+

We recommend registering as soon as you confirm your pregnancy — ideally within the first 8–12 weeks. Early antenatal registration allows us to identify any risks early and create the best care plan for you and your baby.

Is the facility equipped for emergency caesarean sections?
+

Yes. We have a fully operational theatre with trained surgeons, anaesthesiologists, and theatre nurses available 24/7. Emergency C-sections can be performed within minutes of the decision being made.

Do you accept NHIS (National Health Insurance)?
+

Yes, we are a registered NHIS facility. We also accept various HMO plans. Please bring your NHIS card or HMO details at registration. For patients without insurance, we offer transparent pricing and flexible payment arrangements.
