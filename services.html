Our Services

Complete care from
conception to childhood

Eleven specialised services designed around the unique needs of mothers, newborns, and families.

🤰

Antenatal Care

Comprehensive prenatal monitoring from your first positive pregnancy test through the final trimester. We provide regular check-ups, blood pressure monitoring, foetal heart rate checks, nutritional counselling, and birth-plan consultations. Our midwives and obstetricians work together to ensure every pregnancy is closely tracked.

Booking Consultation Growth Monitoring Blood Tests Antenatal Classes Birth Planning
👶

Delivery Services

We support natural vaginal births, water births, assisted deliveries (ventouse/forceps), and emergency or elective caesarean sections. Our delivery suites are equipped with modern foetal monitors, CTG machines, and are staffed by experienced midwives and obstetricians around the clock.

Normal Delivery Caesarean Section Assisted Delivery High-Risk Delivery 24/7 Availability
🍼

Postnatal Care

The period following delivery is critical for mother and baby. Our postnatal team provides wound care, breastfeeding support, newborn assessments, maternal mental health screening, and post-operative monitoring. We ensure every mother leaves confident, healthy, and prepared.

Mother Recovery Breastfeeding Support Newborn Assessment Mental Health Check Wound Care
🧒

Child Healthcare (Paediatrics)

From newborn check-ups to managing childhood illnesses, our paediatric team provides complete child healthcare. We monitor growth and development, manage common conditions, and offer specialist referrals when needed.

Newborn Check-up Growth Monitoring Developmental Screening Sick Child Clinic
💉

Immunization (Vaccination Clinic)

We administer the full National Immunization Schedule plus optional vaccines. Our vaccination clinic runs twice weekly and our trained nurses ensure every injection is safe, properly recorded, and documented in your child's health card.

NPI Schedule BCG, OPV, DPT Hepatitis B Yellow Fever Meningitis Vaccine
🔬

Ultrasound Services

Our on-site ultrasound suite provides 2D and 3D/4D scanning for foetal anomaly detection, growth assessment, Doppler blood flow studies, and ovarian/uterine assessment. Most results are available same-day, reviewed directly by our radiologist.

Dating Scan (6–12 weeks) Anomaly Scan (20 weeks) 3D/4D Scan Doppler Studies Gynaecological Scan
🧫

Laboratory Services

Our on-site laboratory is equipped to process haematology, biochemistry, urinalysis, blood grouping, malaria tests, antenatal screening panels, STI tests, and more. Results for most tests are available within 2–4 hours.

Full Blood Count Antenatal Screen Blood Group & Genotype HIV/STI Testing Urinalysis Malaria Test
💊

Pharmacy Services

Our fully-stocked in-house pharmacy dispenses all prescribed medications, maternity supplements, baby care products, and family planning items. All medications are sourced from NAFDAC-approved suppliers and dispensed by licensed pharmacists.

Prescription Dispensing Maternity Supplements Baby Care Products Counselling Included
🌿

Family Planning

We offer confidential, evidence-based family planning consultations. Our counsellors will help you understand your options — from oral contraceptives and injectables to IUDs, implants, and natural family planning methods.

Oral Contraceptives Injectable Contraceptives IUD / IUCD Implants Natural Methods
🩺

General Healthcare

Beyond maternity, we provide general outpatient consultations, management of chronic conditions like hypertension and diabetes, and treatment of common illnesses including malaria, typhoid, respiratory infections, and skin conditions.

GP Consultation Chronic Disease Management Malaria & Typhoid Wound Dressing Minor Surgery
🚑

Emergency Obstetric Care

Emergencies can arise without warning. Our Emergency Obstetric Care unit handles eclampsia, haemorrhage, obstructed labour, ruptured membranes, premature labour, and neonatal emergencies — 24 hours a day, every day.

24/7 Emergency Eclampsia Management Haemorrhage Control Premature Labour Neonatal Resuscitation

Not sure which service you need?

Our care coordinators will help you find the right service and get you booked quickly.
