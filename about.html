Who We Are

Born from a calling to serve

Magnificent God Maternity Center was founded in 2005 by Dr. (Mrs.) Ogunfunmilayo Mulikat, a dedicated obstetrician who saw the urgent need for a maternity facility that combined clinical excellence with genuine compassion.

What began as a modest 12-bed unit has grown into a fully-equipped maternity and general healthcare centre serving thousands of families every year. Our name reflects our faith and our purpose — that every life is a gift from God, and every patient deserves magnificent care.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide every mother and child with safe, compassionate, and world-class healthcare — regardless of background.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the most trusted maternity and family healthcare center in Lagos, known for excellence, dignity, and outcomes.
